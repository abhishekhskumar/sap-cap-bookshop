Context & Capability US Use Tax — The Problem We're Solving
① The Challenge
US sales & use tax spans 12,000+ jurisdictions with constantly-changing rules. Per Vertex’s 2025 report, 2025 saw rate/rule changes across 12,414 jurisdictions and 335 new taxing jurisdictions — a 10-year high. Rules vary by product, service, and location; software alone doesn’t guarantee accuracy — clean, correct input is the hard part.
② Deployed Today
Doc AI extracts invoices → Vertex applies jurisdiction rules. Proven and in production — but extraction errors flow through to the tax engine uncaught.
③ This Version Adds
A reasoning layer (Doc AI + Claude, cross-checked) that validates, corrects, and verifies extraction before it reaches Vertex — the same proven engine, augmenting rather than replacing it. Plus engine-agnostic output so any tax engine can consume it.
Jurisdiction figures: Vertex Inc., 2025 Sales Tax Rates and Rules Report.
⚡ Three-layer extraction intelligence — SAP Document AI extracts (fast OCR baseline); Claude via SAP AI Core audits each field, corrects the risky ones, and validates against SAP asset master data; Claude Vision (beta) reads the invoice image directly for scanned documents. Per-field routing shows exactly which layer resolved each value.
⚠️ POC data notice: Document AI extraction and the Claude intelligence audit are LIVE. Vertex jurisdiction tax, reconciliation amounts, and SAP asset/PO data are MOCKED for demonstration — marked MOCK where they appear.
